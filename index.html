About Us

Your Community Digital Skills Partner

KASI TECH HUB (PTY) LTD is a community-based digital skills training centre located in Refilwe, Cullinan. We understand the challenges faced by job seekers, students, and small business owners in our community — and we are here to help.

Our training is practical, step-by-step, and designed for people with little or no computer experience. We do not just teach — we sit with you until your CV is done, your application is submitted, and your confidence is built.

The Challenge

Many People in Our Community Are Being Left Behind

Without basic digital skills, accessing jobs, education, and business opportunities is nearly impossible. Here are the barriers we see every day:

😰

No Computer Confidence

Many people have never used a computer before and feel too scared to even try.

🌐

Difficulty Applying Online

Most jobs and bursaries now require online applications — and many people do not know where to start.

📄

Poor or No CV

Without a professional CV, even the most qualified candidates struggle to get interviews.

📝

Confusing Z83 Forms

Government job applications require Z83 forms — which are confusing without proper guidance.

📣

Limited Business Promotion

Small business owners cannot promote their services online and miss out on customers.

Our Solution

We Provide Simple, Step-by-Step Support

We provide simple, step-by-step training and real support so learners can complete actual applications, create CVs, and build confidence. You are not alone — we work with you every step of the way.
